Information We Collect

A. Audio Recording and Transcription Data

Our core service involves audio recording and transcription. Here's what you need to know:

  • Audio recordings are only initiated with your explicit consent
  • Recordings are processed locally on your device first
  • Transcriptions are generated using state-of-the-art AI technology
  • All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption
  • We never record audio in the background or without your knowledge

User Responsibilities for Recordings

As a user of SoulSync, you are responsible for:

  • Using recordings solely for your personal use and legitimate purposes
  • Obtaining explicit consent from all participants before recording any conversations or meetings
  • Informing all parties when recording is active or about to begin
  • Complying with all applicable laws and regulations regarding audio recording in your jurisdiction
  • Respecting the privacy rights of others and not using recordings for any malicious or harmful purposes

Please note that SoulSync does not assume responsibility for users' failure to obtain proper consent or misuse of recordings. It is your responsibility to ensure all parties are aware they are being recorded and have consented to the recording.

B. User-Provided Information

  • Account information (name, email, etc.)
  • Communication with our support team
  • User preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Providing and improving our transcription services
  • Processing your audio recordings into text
  • Maintaining and enhancing app functionality
  • Communication about service updates
  • Customer support

Data Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls for all user data
  • Automatic data deletion policies

Third-Party AI Services

We use industry-leading AI services for transcription. Important notes:

  • Data sent to AI providers is not used for training
  • All third-party processing is governed by strict data protection agreements
  • Transcription data is automatically deleted after 30 days

Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access your recorded data at any time
  • Delete any recordings immediately
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Request complete account deletion

Data Retention

We maintain clear data retention policies:

  • Audio recordings are retained only as long as necessary for transcription
  • Transcriptions are stored for 30 days by default
  • Account data is maintained only while your account is active
  • You can request complete data deletion at any time

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or maintain data from children.

International Data Transfers

If you're using our services from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.
